xcorso — Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

What xcorso is

xcorso is a free orienteering course-planning tool. You can use it without an account. Creating an account enables cloud sync so your projects are available across devices.

This service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data controller

Matteo Guareschi — matteo.guareschi@gmail.com

What we store and why

When you create an account we process the following data to provide the service (legal basis: performance of contract, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR):

  • Your email address — used only for login codes and to identify your account.
  • Your project data (course layouts, control positions) and map files you upload — stored on Cloudflare R2 in Europe.
  • A session cookie (JWT) — strictly necessary for authentication. It expires when you sign out. No other cookies are used.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for anything other than providing the service. We do not run analytics or tracking. We do not access your projects unless required to debug a problem you report.

Retention

Your data is kept until you delete your account. There is no automatic expiry.

Sub-processors

Your data is stored on Cloudflare infrastructure (R2 storage, KV, Workers). Login emails are sent via Resend. No other third parties receive your data.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your personal data. You can exercise these directly in the app:

  • Export — download your projects as .oco files at any time.
  • Delete your account — available in the app. This permanently removes your email, all projects, and all map files from our servers. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

For any other data request, email matteo.guareschi@gmail.com.

If you believe your data is being mishandled you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. For Italy: Garante per la protezione dei dati personali. For the UK: Information Commissioner's Office.

Acceptable use

Don't use xcorso to store illegal content or abuse the service (automated bulk requests, etc.). We may suspend accounts that do.

No warranty

xcorso is provided as-is. We do our best to keep it running and your data safe, but make no guarantees. Back up important projects by downloading .oco files.

Changes

If we update these terms we'll note the date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
